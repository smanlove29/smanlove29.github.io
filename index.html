Who Am I?

About Me

Hello! I'm Sarah. I am finishing up undergrad at the University of Massachusetts Amherst with a degree in Informatics. I am also completing a certificate in Professional Writing and Technical Communication. I have been organizing hackathons like Hack(H)er413 for two years. I am passionate about ethics and diversity in the tech industry.

I had the opportunity to study abroad at the University of Sussex in Brighton, UK in the fall of 2019. This experience has strengthened my love of traveling and of learning new things. I especially love trying new foods! Besides traveling, my hobbies include baking, reading scifi/fantasy, and amateur astronomy.

Hack(H)er413

Hack(H)er413 is the first all-women (cis and trans) and non-binary students' hackathon in Western Massachusetts. I have been involved since its conception in summer of 2018. I have worked as both the Webmaster and the Head of Technology & Operations. Hack(H)er413 has had two successful events, serving 300 students each February. One of my jobs as a student organizer is to build the website each year. Check it out!
