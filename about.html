About Me

Hello! I'm Sarah. I am finishing up undergrad at the University of Massachusetts Amherst with a degree in Informatics. I am also completing a certificate in Professional Writing and Technical Communication. I have been organizing hackathons like HackHer413 for two years. I am passionate about ethics and diversity in the tech industry.

I had the opportunity to study abroad at the University of Sussex in Brighton, UK in the fall of 2019. This experience has strengthened my love of traveling and of learning new things. I especially love trying new foods! Besides traveling, my hobbies include baking, reading scifi/fantasy, and amateur astronomy.
